My Experiences

Co-founder of AashaEd

AashaEd had the moto of 'inclusivity for all' using technology. As the Co-Founder of AashaEd along with my partner Priyanshu Singh, under the guidance of Ms. Deepa Sharma, HOD- Computer Science at Seth Anandram Jaipuria School, we created several GUI based applications which would be able to help people with motor disabilities, learning disabilities and other impairments to be able to learn and communicate to be finally be able to lead a life of opportunities. Our most prominent application was the 'AashaEd Learning Software' which allowed people with learning disabilities and primary-class students to learn to read, spell, pronounce, comprehend and pick-up the use of English words through the usage in a sentence with our software. It was fabricated in the form of a game, comprising of points and difficulty levels. It also allowed the user to track their progress through graphs. It was also tested with people having learning disabilities in our institution, and their review was very affirmative. We have showcased and applied our application in various tech events ever since.

As the Head of Programming at Cyber Crew, it was my job to connect with students all throughout India and abroad who would be participating in the much awaited Tech-Cultural Fest, Compufest, organized by Seth Anandram Jaipuria School, Ghaziabad.
